Internship

Overall Learnings

During our time in internship, it made me realize that working in a professional IT company is not that easy. Adopting changes and handling pressures is needed during this time. I realized that my skills about the program I took during college is not that enough. Self-learning and consistency is also a factor in adopting to a professional IT company. Overall, the experience is fun and challenging. I learned a lot specially on how an IT company works. It gave me a lot of knowledge and experiences that I needed for my future. It also enhances my patience because of the time travel from my house to our office. I enjoyed sometimes the heavy traffic and riding a train. It encourage me to be more independent and open my eyes about the struggles of commuters.
